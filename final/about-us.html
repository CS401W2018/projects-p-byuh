Our History

Alexander's is a renowned fine dining restaurant nestled within the upscale Ala Moana Center in Honolulu. Since Chef Alexander openened its doors in 2015, the restaurant has become a cornerstone of the island's culinary scene, offering a sophisticated dining experience that celebrates the best of Hawaii's local flavors. We are grateful to be located in the heart of the Ala Moana Shopping Center, providing us an elegant backdrop for guests from near and far seeking an amazing meal.

Over the years, Alexander's has earned a reputation for its commitment to quality and sustainability. We work closely with local farmers and fishermen to source fresh, seasonal ingredients. Since our founding, we have strived to emphasize our flavors, elevate our presentation, and provide exceptional service, Alexander's has consistently recieved recognition from both local and international diners, earning our place as one of Honolulu's premier dining destinations.

Alexander's has also consistently been recognized for its culinary excellence and exceptional dining experience. We have earned multiple accolades, including the prestigious Hale 'Aina Award for Best Service and Best Tasting Menu. Additionally, we have been greatly honored with the 'Ilima Award for Most Romantic Restaurant, further bringing us closer to our goals as a premier dining destination in Honolulu.
