Our History

Alexander's is a renowned fine dining restaurant nestled within the upscale Ala Moana Center in Honolulu. Since Chef Alexander openened its doors in 2015, the restaurant has become a cornerstone of the island's culinary scene, offering a sophisticated dining experience that celebrates the best of Hawaii's local flavors. The sleek, modern interior pairs perfectly with its prime, commerical location, providing an elegant backdrop for guests from near and far seeking an amazing meal.

Over the years, Alexander's has earned a reputation for its commitment to quality and sustainability. The restaurant works closely with local farmers and fishermen to source fresh, seasonal ingredients. Emphasizing elevated presentation and exceptional service, Alexander's has consistently recieved recognition from both local and international diners, earning its place as one of Honolulu's premier dining destinations.

Alexander's has consistently been recognized for its culinary excellence and exceptional dining experience. The restaurant has earned multiple accolades, including the prestigious Hale 'Aina Award for Best Service and Best Tasting Menu. Additionally, it has been honored with the 'Ilima Award for Most Romantic Restaurant, further solidifying its status as a premier dining destination in Honolulu.
