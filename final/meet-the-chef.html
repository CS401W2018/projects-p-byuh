A Culinary Journey

Born and raised in the heart of Tuscany, Chef Alexander's culinary journey began in the rolling hills of his family's farm, where he learned to appreciate the rich, authentic flavors of Italian cuisine. His passion for cooking was sparked early by his grandmother, who taught him the art of handmade pasta and the delicate balance of herbs and spices that define Italian cooking.

After years of honing his craft in some of Italy's most renowned kitchens, Chef Alexander's ambition led him to venture beyond his homeland. His search for new culinary horizons brought him to Makiki in Honolulu, Hawaiʻi. It was here he was captivated by island's diverse blend of people and their multicultural influences.

Chef Alexander's dedication to his craft and unwavering commitment to quality food has earned him a Michelin star, a testament to his reputation as one of the most talented chefs here in Honolulu. Every one of his dishes tells the story of his roots, his journey, and his love for both Italy and Oʻahu.
