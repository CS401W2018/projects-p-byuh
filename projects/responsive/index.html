The Tanner Family

Along with Danny's friend, Joey, and Jesse's wife, Becky.

Danny Tanner

Danny Tanner is the loving and kind father of three daughters. As a morning show host and a bit of a neat freak, he brings structure and warmth to his family. This and his corny jokes with his big heart make him the ultimate TV dad.

Jesse Katsopolis

Jesse Katsopolis is Danny's brother-in-law and the ultimate ''cool uncle''. He is probably best described by his love for Elvis and a rock musician personality that differentiates him from Danny. Despite his cool guy exterior, he has a big heart and deeply loves his family.

D.J. Tanner

D.J. Tanner is Danny's eldest daughter and a responsible big sister. She has to balance school, friendships, and family life meanwhile having to set an example for her younger sisters. She has kindness, humor, along with typical teenage struggles. She's very relatable.
