How food brings us together

Food is a universal language, that travels beyond borders bringing people together through shared flavors and traditions. Every meal tells a story, reflecting history, culture, and the values of a community.

From a simple act of breaking bread with strangers to passing down cherished family dishes, the act of sharing a meal strengthns relationships and preserves our traditions Food connects us all, no matter who we are.

Absolute vs Relative Links

A relative link redirects to a location relative of the root directory, typically to another webpage on your site. You use a relative link when linking to a page within your website/domain, for example: Profile Page. An absolute link contains the Protocol (https://), Domain (byuh.edu), and Path (/cs-140). For example: https://github.com/.
