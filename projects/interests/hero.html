Anthony Bourdain was a chef, writer, and documentary maker who used food as a way to illustrate cultures worldwide. Through his shows like Anthony Bourdain: Parts Unknown, he explored the stories, struggles, and traditions of people through their food and cuisine.

If I'm an advocate for anything, it's to move. As far as you can, as much as you can. Across the ocean, or simply across the river. The extent to which you can walk in someone else's shoes or at least eat their food, it's a plus for everybody.

Open your mind, get up off the couch, move.
— Anthony Bourdain
