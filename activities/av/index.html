Engagement Questions

  1. What are the positives and negatives of hosting your videos on YouTube?

    YouTube is a method to freely host your videos on the internet without taking up space on your webserver. However, you have no control over the ads or recommended videos YouTube shows along with your videos (unless you pay YouTube money for no ads).

  2. What is the most useful thing you can do with a transform?

    With transform you can rotate or translate different html elements. When used with transition, you can create simple animations for your elemnts.

  3. How do you stay safe online?

    Implementing and using websites with HTTPS (Http Secure). This means your data and web traffic is encrypted while in transit. This works through a public/private key pair, in which upon connection the public keys are exchanged and certfied by a certificate authority. With the keys verified, the web traffic is encrypted with the public key and decrypted with the private key.
