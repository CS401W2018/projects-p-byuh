Engagement Questions

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

You cannot add width, height, margin, border, etc. to inline tags.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Margin is the spacing outside the element, padding is the spacing within the element, and border is the outline of the element.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

  • px: pixels, which is dependent on the user's display..
  • in: inches, which is a fixed length.
  • cm: centimeters, which is a fixed length.
  • em: element, relative to the size of the element.
  • %: percentage of the display.

When would you use a figure tag?

The figure is a block tag used to markup a image/photo. The figure tag dominates the horizontal space of the page, preventing other elements from being inline with the photo.
