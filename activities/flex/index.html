2. Why do you need to have an accessible website? What does that mean?

We have to accomodate users with disabilities. These disabilities may include vision impairment (can't see the screen well) and motar impairment (can't use a mouse or touch screen).

A website, especially those used for commercial and education purposes, should be designed with functionality included to make the website accomodate such people with disabilities.
